About Me

Hello and welcome to my page! My name is Caleb and I am 29 years old. I am originially from the East Coast but moved to the Pacific North West in the summer of 2020. I love being outdoors in nature hiking on trails. My other favorite thing to do is relax at home, nothing beats a day of video games & youtube. Also, I am married to my lovely partner Seth, they are working on becoming a teacher.

I have been around PCs nearly my whole life. From an early age the technology made me curious about the possibilities. I took a programming course in high school to begin learning some basics. Unfortunately, I did not continue my learning until a few months before starting at Epicodus. I started the CS50 course that Harvard has for free online. I only got a few weeks in before the burnout from the busy season at work hit. That's when I did some searching and found out about Epicodus. I started the course March 14, 2022 and this is my first independent project!
